Bank Bailout Rules

Objective

Accumulate the most Stability Points (SP) by negotiating deals and making strategic decisions to prevent bank collapses.

Gameplay

  1. Draw Phase: The active player draws the top card.
  2. Discussion Phase: Players discuss potential strategies and negotiations regarding the revealed bank for 30 seconds.
  3. Betting Phase: Players secretly decide their SP contribution towards saving the bank, up to their current SP total.
  4. Reveal Phase: Players reveal their bets simultaneously.
  5. Resolution Phase:

    • Bank Collapses: If combined SPs < bank's value, the bank collapses and players lose their bet SPs.

End Game

The game lasts 5 rounds. The player with the highest SP total wins.
